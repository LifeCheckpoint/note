多元函数的极限

    设 `alpha, beta ge 0`, `alpha + beta gt 1`, 则 `(x,y) to (0,0)` 时, `x^alpha y^beta = o(sqrt(x^2+y^2))`,
    `x^alpha y^beta = o(root 4(x^4+y^4))`,
    `x^(n alpha) y^(m beta) = o(sqrt(x^(2n)+y^(2m)))`, `n, m = 1, 2, cdots`.     所以 `(x, y) to (0, 0)` 时, `x y`, `x^2`, `x^(1+p)` (`p gt 0`) 等函数皆是 `sqrt(x^2+y^2)` 的高阶无穷小. 又如, `x y = (x^(3/4) y^(3/4))^(4/3) = o(root 4(x^4+y^4))^(4/3)` `= o(root 3(x^4+y^4))`.
    记 `r = sqrt(x^2+y^2)`, `p = alpha + beta - 1 gt 0`.
  1. 对任意 `epsi gt 0`, 取 `delta = epsi^(1/p)`, 则由 `|x|, |y| le r` 知, 当 `0 lt r lt delta` 时 `|x^alpha y^beta|/sqrt(x^2+y^2) le r^(alpha+beta-1) lt delta^p = epsi`.
  2. 由不等式 `2(x^4 + y^4) ge (x^2+y^2)^2 = r^4`, `|x^alpha y^beta|/root 4(x^4+y^4)` `le r^(alpha+beta)/root 4(1/2 r^4)` `= root 4 2 r^(alpha+beta-1) to 0`.
  3. 由不等式 `|x| le root(2n)(x^(2n)+y^(2m))`, `|y| le root(2m)(x^(2n)+y^(2m))`, 又注意到 `max{|x|, |y|} le r le 1` 时有 `x^(2n)+y^(2m) le x^2+y^2 = r^2`, 得 `(|x|^(n alpha) |y|^(m beta))/(sqrt(x^(2n)+y^(2m)))` `= (x^(2n)+y^(2m))^(alpha/2 + beta/2 - 1/2)` `le r^(alpha+beta-1) to 0`.
